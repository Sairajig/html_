Vidhyalakshmi

Science Examination

Class:10th Standard

Note:Attend all the questions

Exam Date: 05/02/2025

Time: 3 Hours

Max Marks: 100

Section A (1 × 10 = 10 Marks)

Answer the following questions in one word or one sentence:

  1. What is the chemical formula of water?
  2. Name the gas used by plants for photosynthesis.
  3. What is the SI unit of force?
  4. Which planet is known as the "Red Planet"?
  5. Define Newton’s First Law of Motion.

Section B (5 × 3 = 15 Marks)

Answer the following questions in short:

  1. Explain the structure of an atom.
  2. What are the differences between a convex and a concave lens?
  3. Describe the role of mitochondria in a cell.

Section C (5 × 5 = 25 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail:

  1. Describe the process of digestion in the human body with a labeled diagram.
  2. Explain the reflection and refraction of light with examples.
  3. What are renewable and non-renewable sources of energy? Give examples.
  4. Discuss the structure and function of the human heart.
  5. Explain the greenhouse effect and its impact on the environment.

Section D (5 × 10 = 50 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail with diagrams where necessary:

  1. What are the three states of matter? Explain with examples.
  2. Describe the process of reproduction in plants and animals.
  3. Explain the working of an electric circuit and Ohm’s Law.
  4. Discuss the impact of global warming on climate change.
  5. Describe the functions of the nervous system in humans.
